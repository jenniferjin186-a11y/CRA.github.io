Common Challenges During Development

Unclear Requirements

Lack of clear specifications often leads to design changes and delays.

Supplier Communication Issues

Miscommunication with suppliers can result in incorrect manufacturing outcomes.

Prototype Delays

Tooling or material issues can slow down prototype development.

Production Readiness Problems

Gaps in testing or process planning can affect mass production readiness.
